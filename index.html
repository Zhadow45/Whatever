A Collection of 10,000 Oropa

Oropa are a collection of 10,000 that are made up of hundreds of exciting visual trait. Hand-drawn Oropa include skellys, cats, aliens, apes and mascots. The Oropa collection also includes dozens of rare heads, costumes, and colorways of the artist's palette.

OROPA is a community-owned collectibles project showcasing art by The Niece redesigned. OROPA come in a cutesy array of fits, colors and emotions with a collection size of 10,000. Owning an OROPA gives the holder automatic rights to future free airdrops of OROPA seasonal events.
