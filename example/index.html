Discover Oropa
A Collection of 10,000 Oropa
Oropa are a collection of 10,000 NFTs (non-fungible tokens) that are made up of hundreds of exciting visual traits designed by Burnt Toast. Hand-drawn Oropa include skellys, cats, aliens, apes and mascots. The Oropa collection also includes dozens of rare heads, costumes, and colorways of the artist's palette.The Oropa universe is ever-expanding and new experiences like Space Oropa are only available to collectors. While the universe expands, our brand grows, and collectors can expect exclusive access to the latest products, merchandise and events through ownership.
